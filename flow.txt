-- How to make an order?
-- Welcome and show the options
-- The customer pick the option up and send the option number
-- Send the description of the option and ask to confirm
-- The customer send 1 to confirm or 2 to decline 
-- If confirmed, send the total amount; if declined, send the options again
-- If declined, the customer will send the option again 
-- Send the description of the option and ask to confirm
-- The customer send 1 to confirm or 2 to decline (and the process continue until get the right order item)
-- Ask for the address where the order will be sent
-- The customer will send the address
-- Once the address is confirmed, send the payment method options
-- The customer will pick up one option (debit or credit)
-- Send the link for payment and the order number
